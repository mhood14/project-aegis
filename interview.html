Interview Flow

A concise walkthrough of the system and its security decisions.

This flow is meant to explain what was built, how the controls work, and where the hardest risks are. It starts with architecture, moves through identity and retrieval, and ends with threat priorities and operations.

01

Open with the deployment boundary

Describe the hub-and-spoke layout, the separation between app, AI, and data services, and where private endpoints and private DNS fit into the design.

02

Explain identity and scope enforcement

Walk through Entra ID sign-in, Conditional Access, MFA, RBAC, and why server-side scope validation is the most important trust control in the application.

03

Show the document lifecycle

Cover upload, raw storage, chunk processing, retrieval indexing, cited responses, and how only selected context reaches Azure OpenAI.

04

Discuss the main AI-specific risks

Use prompt injection, knowledge poisoning, secret exposure attempts, and false authorization claims to show why document trust has to stay separate from model confidence.

05

Close with monitoring and future hardening

Point to Sentinel, Log Analytics, Defender-related diagnostics, and the planned move toward centralized Azure Firewall egress enforcement.
